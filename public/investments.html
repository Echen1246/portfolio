Investments

Philosophy

I invest in companies building transformative technology, particularly AI, robotics, and automation. Long-term focus, buying businesses I want to own for years.

What I Look For

  • Strong leadership and vision
  • Defensible competitive advantages
  • Growing addressable markets
  • Reasonable valuations
  • Potential for long-term compounding

Focus Areas

AI Infrastructure: Chips, cloud services, foundational models

Robotics: Physical AI and automation

Energy: Clean energy and storage

Space: Commercial space ventures

Approach

Buy and hold. Focus on fundamentals, not market timing. Read annual reports, understand the business, think in decades not quarters.

Learning

  • The Intelligent Investor - Benjamin Graham
  • Zero to One - Peter Thiel
  • The Innovator's Dilemma - Clayton Christensen
  • Berkshire Hathaway letters

Not investment advice. Do your own research.
